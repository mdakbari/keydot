Our Studio

Keydot, a graphic design studio based in Ahmedabad, India, founded by Krupa Gajera. Specialized in branding, the focus of our work is to develop visual identities, through printed and digital solutions for any kind of client. We explore concepts, to relate customer and company, having as a principle the clients claims, always favoring direct contact with them. We help brands tell their story.

Collaboration is at the heart of what we do. Our team thrives on the synergy that arises when unique perspectives converge, fostering an environment of boundless creativity. By harnessing our collective expertise, we produce extraordinary results that consistently surpass expectations
